My Web Development Journey

From Beginner to Pro: My Path in Web Development

Web development wasn’t always my career choice, but once I discovered the power of creating something that millions could access, I knew I had found my passion. Here's a glimpse of how I turned that passion into a career.

My Starting Point

My journey began in my free time, experimenting with HTML and CSS. I didn’t have formal education in computer science, but I found countless resources online to help me. Slowly, I began to build simple static websites.

Expanding My Skillset

Once I understood the basics, I ventured into JavaScript and started building more dynamic web pages. Learning React and Node.js allowed me to work on full-stack projects, and soon I was working with clients on real-world applications.

Notable Projects

  • E-commerce platform for a local store
  • Personal portfolio website
  • Interactive dashboard for data analytics

Future Goals

My aim is to continue expanding my knowledge in AI-driven development and to contribute to open-source projects that help other developers grow.
